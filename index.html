Welcome to The House of Barberium

Tradition Meets Modern Craft

Located on Market Street in the heart of Hoylake, The House of Barberium is a premium barbershop where classic grooming traditions meet modern style.

From precision skin fades to luxurious hot towel shaves, every service is delivered with care, skill, and attention to detail. Whether you're after a sharp new look or a relaxing grooming experience, you're in expert hands.

Walk-ins welcome. Appointments available.

— Ramazan Demirci, Founder
